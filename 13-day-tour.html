13-Day Grand Morocco Tour from Casablanca

Published on October 22, 2024 | Category: Grand Tours

For those who want to see it all, our 13-day grand tour is the definitive Moroccan journey. Starting and ending in Casablanca, this itinerary covers the imperial cities, the Rif Mountains, the vast Sahara desert, the High Atlas, and the Atlantic coast. It's a comprehensive and immersive experience of Morocco's incredible diversity.

Abbreviated Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Casablanca.
  • Day 2: Casablanca - Rabat - Chefchaouen.
  • Day 3: Exploring Chefchaouen (The Blue City).
  • Day 4: Chefchaouen - Volubilis - Meknes - Fes.
  • Day 5: Fes Guided Tour.
  • Day 6: Fes - Ifrane - Midelt - Ziz Valley - Merzouga.
  • Day 7: Sahara Desert Exploration & Camel Trek.
  • Day 8: Merzouga - Todra Gorges - Dades Valley.
  • Day 9: Dades Valley - Ouarzazate - Ait Benhaddou - Marrakech.
  • Day 10-11: Exploring Marrakech.
  • Day 12: Day trip to Essaouira on the Atlantic Coast.
  • Day 13: Departure from Marrakech or Casablanca.

Why Choose This Tour?

This is the ultimate trip for travelers who want a deep dive into Moroccan culture and geography. You'll experience the stark contrasts between the bustling cities and the tranquil desert, the cool blue hues of Chefchaouen and the warm oranges of the Sahara. It is, without a doubt, the most complete Moroccan adventure we offer.

Did You Know?

The ancient Roman ruins of Volubilis, which you'll visit on this tour, are a UNESCO World Heritage site and feature some of the best-preserved mosaics in North Africa.
