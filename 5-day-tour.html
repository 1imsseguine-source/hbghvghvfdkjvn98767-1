5-Day Tour: Marrakech to Fes via the Sahara Desert

Published on October 24, 2024 | Category: Desert Tours

This one-way journey from Marrakech to Fes is one of our most popular Sahara desert tours, taking you through the most spectacular landscapes in southern Morocco. From the winding roads of the High Atlas to the vast, silent dunes of Erg Chebbi, this tour is a feast for the senses.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech - High Atlas Mountains - Ait Benhaddou - Ouarzazate

Depart from Marrakech and cross the Tizi n'Tichka pass. Visit the iconic Ait Benhaddou kasbah, a famous film location, before arriving in Ouarzazate, the "Hollywood of Africa."

Day 2: Ouarzazate - Dades Valley - Todra Gorges

Travel along the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs, through the Skoura oasis and the Valley of Roses, arriving at the dramatic cliffs of the Todra Gorges.

Day 3: Todra Gorges - Erfoud - Merzouga (Erg Chebbi)

Head towards the desert, passing through the fossil town of Erfoud. In Merzouga, you'll meet your camel caravan for a trek into the dunes, where you'll spend the night in a traditional Berber camp.

Day 4: Merzouga - Ziz Valley - Midelt - Ifrane - Fes

Wake up to a desert sunrise before starting the journey to Fes. Travel through the scenic Ziz Valley and the Middle Atlas Mountains, with a stop in Ifrane, known as "Little Switzerland."

Day 5: Fes Guided Tour

After breakfast, enjoy a full-day guided tour of Fes's historic medina before the tour concludes in the evening.

Tour Inclusions

  • Transportation in a modern 4x4 or minivan
  • English-speaking driver/guide
  • Accommodation for 4 nights
  • Camel ride and overnight stay in a desert camp
  • Breakfasts and some dinners
