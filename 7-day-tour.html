7-Day Trip: Imperial Cities & Sahara Desert Adventure

Published on October 26, 2024 | Category: Desert Tours

This week-long journey is the perfect introduction to the wonders of Morocco. You'll explore ancient medinas, cross the stunning Atlas Mountains, and spend an unforgettable night in a traditional Berber camp under the Saharan stars. It's an adventure that combines culture, history, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Marrakech

Arrive at Marrakech Menara Airport and transfer to your riad in the medina. Spend the day exploring the vibrant Djemaa el-Fna square.

Day 2: Marrakech to Dades Gorges

We travel through the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, visiting the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Benhaddou before continuing to the Dades Valley.

Day 3: Dades Gorges to Merzouga (Sahara Desert)

Journey to the Sahara, stopping at the Todra Gorges. Upon arrival in Merzouga, you'll ride a camel into the Erg Chebbi dunes to watch the sunset and stay overnight in a desert camp.

Day 4: Merzouga to Fes

After sunrise in the desert, we travel north through the Ziz Valley and cedar forests, home to Barbary macaques, arriving in the cultural capital, Fes, in the evening.

Day 5: Exploring Fes

Enjoy a guided tour of Fes, the oldest of the imperial cities. Explore the labyrinthine medina, famous tanneries, and historic madrasas.

Day 6: Fes to Rabat & Casablanca

Travel from Fes to the capital city of Rabat to see the Royal Palace and Hassan Tower, then continue to Casablanca to visit the magnificent Hassan II Mosque.

Day 7: Departure from Casablanca

Transfer to Casablanca Mohammed V International Airport for your departure.

What's Included/Not Included

  • Included: Private A/C vehicle, driver/guide, accommodation, specified meals, camel trek.
  • Not Included: Flights, lunches, drinks, entrance fees, tips.
